Privacy policy

Last updated: March 2026

What we collect

When you contact us through our website or WhatsApp, we collect the information you provide: your name, email address, phone number, and details about your business. We also collect standard analytics data such as page visits and device type through Google Analytics.

How we use it

We use your information only to respond to your enquiry, deliver the services you've hired us for, and send occasional updates if you've agreed to this. We do not use your data for any other purpose.

What we never do

  • We never sell your data to any third party.
  • We never share your information with advertisers.
  • We never send unsolicited marketing messages.
  • We never store payment information on our servers.

Third-party services

Our website uses Google Analytics for anonymous traffic analysis and Google Fonts for typography. These services may collect standard browser data under their own privacy policies. Ad campaigns we manage on your behalf on Meta, TikTok, or Google operate under those platforms' respective terms.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you at any time by emailing hello@zuralogix.com. We will respond within 7 business days.
